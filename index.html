About Game:

Thank you for playing Winden! This game is a product of love by James Summersby (me!), a software development student. It is born from my love and passion for story driven games and, most importantly, it is adapted from the Netflix show Dark. I thoroughly enjoyed watching this show in early 2021 and, when deciding for a suitable story to adapt for this project, it was the obvious choice. If you enjoyed this game's story, I highly recommend giving it a watch.
